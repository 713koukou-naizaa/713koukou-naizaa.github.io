Description

Comus Party is a web application offering various online mini-games. Though I didn't take part in the initial conception, I joined the group in my 2nd year in order to study agile methods and SCRUM as well as develop the application.

Songs market
Description

Songs market is a prototype of a web application that allows users to buy songs. The application does not aim to be deployed and fully functional. It consists of a collection of songs that can be added to the cart, with a page to visualize it. It also contains a back-office page to add, remove, and modify songs.

Book'n'Go
Description

Book'n'Go is a web application I worked on with a group of 4 during my first year of computer science at the IUT de Bayonne. Its purpose is to help travelers in Bayonne organize their trips while allowing them to share their experiences. This project was first thought of as a learning experience to become accustomed to the conception and management part of a project.
