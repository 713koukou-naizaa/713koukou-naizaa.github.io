Book'n'Go

Book'n'Go is a web app we designed from scratch for a project to practice project management. We first had to think about the goals of an app define the primary features, think about the future main users of our app, create use case diagrams and scenarios, and mock-ups. We also held multiple team-meetings as well as with our tutor and drew up many reports.

Figma

We created a team on Figma to work together on the tens of wireframe mock-ups for the different scenarios we imagined for the personas of our app.

GanttProject

The group used GanttProject to plan the whole project, its milestones and tasks.

UML and Modelio

UML was used with Modelio to create the different use case diagrams and therefore plan the primary features.
